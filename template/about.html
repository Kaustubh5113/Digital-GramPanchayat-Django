The ‘Digital Gram Panchayat’ is a software,which is essential to support Digital India making Rural people's life easy and hassle-free, and helping them in development.

The Gram Panchayat software is specially developed for Gram Panchayat to support Digital India. It helps me to run all Gram Panchayat's operations smoothly and makes the process very easy. All data is safe and secured in the software, and helps you to operate anywhere as it supports web - based applications. All important documents and processes are hassle - free such as birth, marriage, death certificates, various bill request and many other types of all certificates which can be issued & generated by Gram Panchayat as per government format.

A click is all it takes to manage the software with a simple user - friendly interface, and to streamline operations smoothly.
